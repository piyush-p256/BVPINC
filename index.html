About Us

BVPINC is a professional college society dedicated to fostering leadership, innovation, and professional development among students. We organize workshops, seminars, and networking events to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.

Our Events

Survival Showdown 2023

"The Survival Showdown" tested participants' persuasive skills and strategic thinking across challenging rounds. The goal was to outwit opponents and win over the audience by demonstrating quick thinking, adaptability, and strong rhetorical abilities. The event enhanced public speaking, strategic planning, and personal growth while offering entertainment and community engagement for all.

Mind Maelstrom

"Mind Maelstrom" was an indeathon where participants were given themes on the spot to build and develop their project ideas. The event challenged creativity and quick thinking as teams had to rapidly conceptualize, design, and present their ideas. It fostered innovation, problem-solving, and teamwork, offering participants a chance to showcase their skills and adaptability in a fast-paced, high-pressure environment.

Resume and Linkedin workshop

This workshop focused on helping attendees craft impactful resumes and optimize their LinkedIn profiles. As a society, we aimed to provide practical insights on personal branding and effective networking, equipping participants with the tools to enhance their professional presence and improve their career opportunities.

Idea Roulette

"Idea Roulette" challenged participants to adapt, collaborate, and think critically. In the first round, teams created business ideas based on random themes. In the second round, ideas were shuffled, and teams had to complete others' projects. The event tested adaptability and teamwork, helping participants build skills essential for real-world business scenarios, all while promoting creativity and innovation within our society.

Gareeb Shark Tank

"Gareeb Shark Tank" allowed teams to showcase their creativity by submitting innovative ideas aimed at solving real-world problems. The most promising ventures were selected as finalists, who then pitched their ideas live to a panel of esteemed judges. This event emphasized ingenuity, strategic thinking, and the art of captivating presentations. It offered participants the opportunity to establish themselves as thought leaders, build trust with their target audience, and enhance their industry expertise.

Tech-Yukt

"Tech-Yukt" prepared participants for the corporate world by enhancing their profiles for placements and internships. The event offered real-world scenario preparation, technical assessments, and mock interviews, helping participants improve problem-solving, communication, and critical thinking skills. With expert guidance and no elimination rounds, attendees gained confidence and were better equipped for professional success.

Aptilectual

Aptilectual by BVPInc is an interactive platform where users can solve daily aptitude problems, track their progress, and compete on leaderboards. Designed to sharpen problem-solving skills, Aptilectual offers a fun and engaging way for individuals to enhance their aptitude through consistent practice and friendly competition.
